Jimmy Buffett is known for his beach music and trop rock. Jimmy Buffett is not yacht rock. If you want to know about yacht rock, click the link above.

Is Jimmy Buffett yacht rock? The answer is no. Yacht rock is a smooth, jazz-infused style of soft rock from the late 70s and early 80s, featuring artists like Michael McDonald, Steely Dan, Toto, and Christopher Cross. Jimmy Buffett’s tropical island-themed music falls under “Gulf and Western” rather than yacht rock. Learn more about the genre and its defining artists at Smooth Sailing Radio.
